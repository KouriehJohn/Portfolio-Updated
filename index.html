IDE'S

Visual Studio, Sublime, etc.

Development Enviroments

I've tried my fair share of code editors from light weight, to Visual Studio. They all have their positives and negatives. Some get in your way, while others help but take a long time to set up. Here's some details about a few IDE's I have tried.

  • Visual Studio Code
    - Very fast and easy to edit with. Like anything else MicroSoft, it does have its moods.
  • Atom
    -A beautiful and helpful editor, although it is slower to load and sometimes its plugins are in my way.
  • Sublime Text
    - Probably, my favorite editor. It's quick and doesn't get in your way. An added plus is its universal across platforms.
  • Dreamweaver
    - A great tool with its own unique style and feel. Quite premium!
